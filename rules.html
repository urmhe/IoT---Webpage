Rules

General Overview

The goal of the game is to earn as many points as possible within the initial time period of 30 seconds.

Points are earned by performing the correct action based on the prompts that you are given. Based on the wording of the prompt you will have to press the correct button or shake your device to earn a point.

Prompts

There are two types of prompts:

  1. Press green
  2. Press the red button

Of course, the prompt text and color is randomised to make the game more difficult.

Prompt type #1

You are expected to press the button that corresponds to the text color that is used within the prompt.

Example: Press orange

To get the point, the green button needs to be pressed.

Prompt type #2

You are expected to press the button that is described by the prompt.

Example: Press the red button

To get the point, the red button needs to be pressed.

Special rule

For both prompt types, if the text is the same as the text color, then instead of pressing a button you have to shake your device.

Example: Press the orange button

To get the point, you have to shake your device.

Note: For devices that do not have the necessary sensors, a shake button will be provided.

Timer and scoring points

The timer initially starts at 30 seconds. If the timer ever hits 0, then the game is over.

Performing the correct action will grant you one point and also increases the remaining time by 1 second.

Performing the wrong action will result in a game over.
